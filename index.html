Home Watch

Your home,
protected
in your absence.

Whether your property is an investment, second home, or you're planning extended absences — our team of certified Home Watch professionals consistently monitors your property and resolves any issues using our in-house maintenance team and extensive network of vetted local service providers.

We work with you to develop a customized schedule that includes property checks to address the specific needs of your home. We visit at regular intervals and communicate regularly so you're always well informed.

Inquire About Home Watch
✓

NHWA Accredited Member — Certified Home Watch Professional (CHWP #12230369). Accredited members must be bonded and insured, background-checked, and adhere to strict ethics and best business practices.

Every Home Check Includes

—Check for unusual odors, noises, or mechanical equipment alarms
—Visual inspection of HVAC, sump pump, and hot water systems
—Full exterior visual inspection
—Check for signs of pest infestation
—Inspect condensers and generator
—Bring mail & packages indoors
—Verify all doors and windows are locked and secured
—Post-storm damage inspection
—Adjust thermostats for arrival or departure
—Detailed report delivered to homeowner after every visit
—Personalized checks as requested by the homeowner
